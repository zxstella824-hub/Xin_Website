Voice Studio

Private Voice Instruction

Welcome to My Voice Studio

I offer private voice instruction for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced performers. With over a decade of teaching experience and ongoing doctoral studies in vocal performance, I bring both expertise and passion to each lesson.

Teaching Experience

  • Current: Graduate Teaching Assistant, Voice Area, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign
  • Teach weekly individual applied voice lessons to undergraduate music-major students
  • Lead and teach studio classes
  • Prepare students for junior and senior voice recitals
  • Organize required studio recitals
  • Private Studio: Teaching since 2015 in Beijing, China and Urbana, IL
  • Successfully prepared students for choir, high school, university, and summer program auditions

Teaching Philosophy

I believe in developing each student's unique voice through personalized instruction. My approach combines:

  • Technical foundation in breath support, vocal production, and healthy singing
  • Musical interpretation and expression
  • Repertoire development tailored to individual goals
  • Performance preparation and confidence building
