Welcome to My Voice Studio

I offer private voice instruction for students of all levels, from beginners to advanced performers. With over a decade of teaching experience and ongoing doctoral studies in vocal performance, I bring both expertise and passion to each lesson.

Teaching Philosophy and Method

My teaching philosophy is grounded in the belief that every voice is unique and deserves to be nurtured with care, patience, and curiosity. I strive to create a supportive environment where singers can build confidence, develop healthy technique, and discover joy in expressive music-making. I see voice teaching not only as a technical process but also as a holistic journey that cultivates body awareness, emotional connection, and artistic growth.

My method focuses on the following:

  1. Breath and Body Awareness: Establishing relaxed but active posture, efficient breath support, and body-mind connection through simple alignment and breath exercises.
  2. Tone and Resonance: Encouraging a free, balanced tone through vocal exercises that promote openness and ease, helping students find resonance without strain.
  3. Range Development and Registration: Helping students understand and navigate vocal registers (chest, head, mix) through gentle vocalises, while making sure they sing in a comfortable, age-appropriate range.
  4. Text and Expression: Encouraging connection to the text through diction work, phrasing, and storytelling. Even early in development, I believe expressive communication builds confidence and connection.
  5. Vocal Health and Repertoire Choices: I emphasize sustainable technique and choose repertoire that supports vocal growth—songs that stretch ability but don't overburden the voice.

I adjust my approach based on each student's voice type, musical background, and learning style. My goal is to help them sing with freedom, authenticity, and growing independence, while building a strong technical foundation and expressive artistry.
